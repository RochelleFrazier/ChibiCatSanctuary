Helping Felines in the State of Pennsylvania


Welcome to Chibi's Cat Sanctuary!

Chibi’s Cat Sanctuary is a facility where felines from the state of Pennsylvania are brought to have a permanent home to live and to be protected for the rest of their lives. But we also offer adoptions to certain adoptable cats. This sanctuary helps families that can no longer take care of their cats due to a death in the family, moving somewhere where felines are not allowed, and just any unwanted cats. We also accept stray and feral felines. We provide a small tour of our facility to allow residents and adopters to see what we offer our feline friends.
